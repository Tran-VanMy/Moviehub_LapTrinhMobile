Base URL: https://api.themoviedb.org/3

Các endpoint trong app:
- Trending movies (lấy phim trending trong ngày): GET /trending/movie/day Link: https://developer.themoviedb.org/reference/trending-all?utm_source=chatgpt.com
- Now Playing (phim đang chiếu tại rạp): GET /movie/now_playing Link: https://developer.themoviedb.org/reference/movie-now-playing-list?utm_source=chatgpt.com
- Top Rated (phim có điểm đánh giá cao): GET /movie/top_rated Link: https://developer.themoviedb.org/docs/getting-started?utm_source=chatgpt.com
- Movie details (chi tiết 1 phim (overview, runtime, genres, backdrop…)): GET /movie/{movie_id} Link: https://developer.themoviedb.org/docs/getting-started?utm_source=chatgpt.com
- Credits (cast) (danh sách diễn viên): GET /movie/{movie_id}/credits Link: https://developer.themoviedb.org/docs/getting-started?utm_source=chatgpt.com
- Videos (trailer) (list trailer/teaser, dùng key YouTube): GET /movie/{movie_id}/videos Link: https://developer.themoviedb.org/docs/getting-started?utm_source=chatgpt.com
- Similar movies (phim tương tự): GET /movie/{movie_id}/similar Link: https://developer.themoviedb.org/docs/getting-started?utm_source=chatgpt.com
- Search (tìm phim theo tên, param query): GET /search/movie Link: https://developer.themoviedb.org/docs/getting-started?utm_source=chatgpt.com
