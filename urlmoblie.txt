Web API Movie TMDB: https://www.themoviedb.org/?language=vi

My API KEY: https://www.themoviedb.org/settings/api

Trang tài liệu chính thức:
- TMDB Getting Started: https://developer.themoviedb.org/docs/getting-started?utm_source=chatgpt.com
- TMDB Authentication (Bearer Token / api_key): https://developer.themoviedb.org/docs/authentication-application?utm_source=chatgpt.com

Base URL: https://api.themoviedb.org/3

Các endpoint trong app:
- Trending movies (lấy phim trending trong ngày): 
GET /trending/movie/day 
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/reference/trending-all?utm_source=chatgpt.com

- Now Playing (phim đang chiếu tại rạp): 
GET /movie/now_playing 
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/reference/movie-now-playing-list?utm_source=chatgpt.com

- Top Rated (phim có điểm đánh giá cao): 
GET /movie/top_rated 
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/docs/getting-started?utm_source=chatgpt.com

- Search movie (tìm kiếm phim theo tên): 
GET /search/movie 
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/reference/search-movie?utm_source=chatgpt.com

- Movie details (chi tiết 1 phim (overview, runtime, genres, backdrop…)): 
GET /movie/{movie_id} 
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/docs/getting-started?utm_source=chatgpt.com

- Credits (cast) (danh sách diễn viên): 
GET /movie/{movie_id}/credits 
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/docs/getting-started?utm_source=chatgpt.com

- Videos (trailer) (list trailer/teaser, dùng key YouTube): 
GET /movie/{movie_id}/videos 
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/docs/getting-started?utm_source=chatgpt.com

- Similar movies (phim tương tự): 
GET /movie/{movie_id}/similar 
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/docs/getting-started?utm_source=chatgpt.com

- Recommendations (You may also like): 
GET /movie/{movie_id}/recommendations 
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/reference/movie-recommendations?utm_source=chatgpt.com

- Genres (Danh sách thể loại): 
GET /genre/movie/list 
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/reference/genre-movie-list?utm_source=chatgpt.com

- Discover Movies (lọc nâng cao: thể loại, năm, rating, sort...): 
GET /discover/movie 
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/reference/discover-movie?utm_source=chatgpt.com

- Popular People (Danh sách người nổi tiếng): 
GET /person/popular 
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/reference/person-popular?utm_source=chatgpt.com

- Person Detail (Chi tiết 1 người nổi tiếng): 
GET /person/{person_id} 
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/reference/person-details?utm_source=chatgpt.com

- Person Movie Credits (Danh sách phim người đó tham gia): 
GET /person/{person_id}/movie_credits 
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/reference/person-movie-credits?utm_source=chatgpt.com

- Search (tìm phim theo tên, param query): 
GET /search/movie 
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/docs/getting-started?utm_source=chatgpt.com

- Reviews (comment thật từ TMDB):
GET /movie/{movie_id}/reviews
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/reference/movie-reviews

- Watch Providers – phim xem ở đâu:
GET /movie/{movie_id}/watch/providers
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/reference/movie-watch-providers

- Popular (phim phổ biến):
GET /movie/popular
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/reference/movie-popular-list

- Upcoming (phim sắp chiếu):
GET /movie/upcoming
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/reference/movie-upcoming-list

- Image Base URL (Lấy poster, backdrop, avatar): 
Link: https://image.tmdb.org/t/p/w500
Docs: https://developer.themoviedb.org/docs/image-basics?utm_source=chatgpt.com
